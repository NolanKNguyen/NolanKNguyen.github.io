Rebuilding Elizabot

Context

My goal was to create a chatbot that feels like a late-night comic who moonlights as a terrible therapist. Nestor still “talks” with the user like the original Elizabot, but he derails the conversation with jokes, sarcasm, and the occasional roast. This makes the bot feel less robotic and more like a chaotic, snarky friend

Overall, Nestor the Comedian is designed to be more entertaining than helpful—and that’s the point. Sometimes, you don’t need a therapist. Sometimes, you just need a sarcastic chatbot who’ll make you laugh at your problems instead of solve them.
